Support

Wardrobe Log helps you log what you buy, see where your money goes, and stay close to your monthly budget. Here are answers to common questions.

How do I add an item?

Tap the + button in the tab bar, fill in at least a price and category, and save. You can also attach a photo and a note.

What is the difference between a purchase and "already owned"?

Items marked as already owned are part of your closet inventory but do not count toward your monthly spending or budget.

How do I set my monthly budget?

Open Settings from the Summary screen and type any amount under "Monthly budget". The Summary uses it to show how much room you have left.

Does my data sync between my devices?

Yes. If you are signed in to iCloud, your closet syncs automatically across your Apple devices. Sync status is shown on the Summary screen.

My data is not showing up on another device.

Make sure both devices are signed in to the same iCloud account and have iCloud Drive enabled, and that you have a network connection. Sync can take a little while; reopening the app usually nudges it along.

How do I change the photo on an item?

Open the item, tap its photo to preview it, then choose Change photo or Remove photo.

How do I delete items?

Touch and hold an item to edit or delete it. To start over, use "Clear all purchases" in Settings.
